MISSION

To provide solution to problems related to deforestration,air polution and industralization by creating awareness on the importance of trees plantation and the general side effects of air polution

VISION

Our vision is to do away with negligence in our society by planting more trees to do away with these dangerous infections in our community associated with air pollution caused by increased industralization and deforestration

EMPOWERMENT

Through afforestration,we help solve problems associated with soil erosion,encourages agricultural activities as these trees attract rainfall,some act as water catchment areas.Moreover soil gain more fatility.We also save on cost of medication as trees clean the atmosphere thus reducing the rate of infections associated with air pollution,for these reasons this make this website more empowering to the less fortunate people in our society and the society as a whole.
